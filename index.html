About

ŞMKMUN is the official Model United Nations conference organized by Şehit Mustafa Kaymakçı Anatolian Imam Hatip High School in collaboration with Miraj Islamic High School. We are immensely grateful for the invaluable contributions and enthusiastic participation of Miraj Islamic High School, which enhances the diversity and richness of our conference.


With a focus on empowering young minds, ŞMKMUN offers an unparalleled opportunity for students to develop critical skills such as research, public speaking, negotiation, and diplomacy. Delegates will have the chance to engage in lively debates, draft resolutions, and collaborate with peers from different backgrounds, all while gaining a deeper appreciation for the complexities of international relations.





Letter from the Director General

Esteemed participants and distinguished guests,

I am honored to extend a warm invitation to all of you to join us at the 1st SMKMUN Conference, which will be held on July 18th, 19th, and 20th. As the Director General, it is my utmost pleasure to welcome you to this esteemed event, where talented young minds from around the world will gather to engage in thought-provoking discussions and simulated diplomatic negotiations.

With great excitement, our dedicated team has meticulously planned an enriching and immersive conference experience for all participants. The SMKMUN'23 Conference will provide an exceptional platform for delegates to develop their skills in diplomacy, critical thinking, public speaking, and negotiation. Our experienced academic team has carefully crafted a diverse range of committees and agenda items, ensuring that delegates will have the opportunity to address pressing global issues and propose innovative solutions.

Beyond the academic aspect, SMKMUN'23 will offer an engaging and vibrant atmosphere that fosters cultural exchange and international friendships. Our social events and cultural activities will provide delegates with the opportunity to connect with peers from different backgrounds, share perspectives, and create lasting memories.

To ensure the smooth functioning of the conference, our organization team has been working tirelessly to handle all logistical aspects, including venue arrangements, accommodation options, and transportation services. We are committed to providing a seamless and comfortable experience for all participants throughout their stay.

I invite you to embrace this unique opportunity to broaden your horizons, challenge your intellect, and make lasting connections with like-minded individuals who share your passion for global affairs. SMKMUN'23 promises to be a memorable and transformative experience for each participant.

Please do not hesitate to reach out to our Secretariat team for any questions or assistance you may require. We are here to support you in making the most of your SMKMUN'23 experience.

On behalf of the entire SMKMUN'23 team, I look forward to welcoming you to our conference and witnessing the invaluable contributions you will make in shaping a brighter future.

Warmest regards,

Enes Bünyamin ÇELİK
Director General
