What is MUN?

Model United Nations is an educational simulation and academic activity through which students can learn about diplomacy, international relations, and the United Nations. Participants in Model United Nations conferences, known as delegates, are placed to a committee and assigned a country that they represent throughout the conference.

Where will the conference be held?

The conference will be held physically in our school campus.

Which MUN procedure SMKMUN follow?

SMKMUN'23 will follow the Harvard MUN procedure.

How can I apply?

You can apply via registration page.

What are the application criteria?

- Being able to understand English properly
- All attendees must apply to our conference on behalf of a school or institution. Participants who do not apply on behalf of an institution or school will not be accepted.

What is the official language of the conference?

The official language of our conference is English.

Will there be accommodation?

Yes, there will be accommodation for the participants who will attend from the international and European side of Istanbul.

Will there be a social event?

Yes, there will be multiple social events waiting for you!
